The specificity score is typically represented as a four-part value: `(a, b, c, d)` a: Inline styles (`style=""` in HTML) have the highest specificity. b: IDs (`#id`) are next in specificity. c: Classes (`.class`), attributes (`[type="text"]`), and pseudo-classes (`:hover`, `:nth-child`) d: Element selectors (e.g., `div`, `p`) and pseudo-elements (`::before`, `::after`) have the lowest specificity.
